About Me

My name is Ayala Kadosi, I have been a kindergarten teacher and instructor for 27 years at the Ministry of Education. This kit was born from my professional experience in my visits to kindergartens and as a kindergarten teacher in a mandatory kindergarten. As part of my doctoral studies, I identified the need for kindergarteners for an educational tool that would help them impart and establish phonological awareness in preparation for first grade, in an experiential way through games in a meeting, small group, and individually. The assessment is intended for kindergarten children, first graders and children in special education who are in the stages of knowing letters and developing auditory skills with the help of phonological awareness.

My vision

to create a safe environment, for the kindergarten to be a place for the children to develop according to their personal way.

Something I learned from the children:

making mistakes is the best learning in life. From mistakes, from difficulties, you can grow and change behaviors.
